Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduMind? +
EduMind is an AI-powered study assistant that helps students manage notes, tasks, timers, and learning tools in one place.
Is EduMind free to use? +
Yes, EduMind offers free access to essential features so students can start learning without any cost.
How do I upload my study notes? +
You can upload PDFs, images, or save links directly from the Notes section in your dashboard.
Can I use EduMind on mobile devices? +
Yes, EduMind is fully responsive and works smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
How does the AI summarizer work? +
The AI summarizer analyzes your notes and generates short, clear summaries to help you understand key concepts faster.
Are my notes and data secure? +
Yes, your data is securely stored using cloud services with proper authentication and protection.
Can I track my study progress? +
EduMind provides progress tracking features so you can monitor completed tasks and study sessions.
What tools are included in EduMind? +
EduMind includes Notes, To-Do List, Pomodoro Timer, Whiteboard, AI tools, and cloud storage features.
